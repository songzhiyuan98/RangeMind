TiltGuard Privacy Policy

Data Collection

TiltGuard does not collect personal information. All poker session data, hand records, and statistics are stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework.

Sign in with Apple

If you choose to sign in with Apple, only the minimal identifier provided by Apple is used for account identification. We do not access or store your email address or personal details beyond what Apple provides.

Analytics & Tracking

TiltGuard does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. No data is transmitted to external servers.

Data Sharing

TiltGuard does not sell, share, or transmit any personal data to third parties.

Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device. If you delete the app, all data will be permanently removed. Guest mode data is not synced and exists only on the device.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
